# Great Southern Bancorp (2023) Performance & Risk Audit

A data-driven performance analysis and risk audit of Great Southern Bancorp for Fiscal Year 2023. This project integrates processing, modeling, and interactive visualization to convert raw operational metrics into actionable corporate strategy.

## Project Overview
The analysis evaluates operations across a 99-branch network by integrating and cross-referencing three primary tracking dimensions:
* **Customer Base:** 5,000 active customer records
* **Transaction Ledger:** 5,000 processed transactions totaling $12.54M in volume
* **Credit Portfolio:** A $127.51M active loan portfolio

## Core Analytical Insights

### 📊 Demographics & Market Expansion
* Isolated strong growth momentum within the youth, female, and West Coast customer segments.
* Defined targeted parameters for localized commercial expansion based on demographic density and transaction frequency.

### 💳 Credit Risk & Portfolio Health
* Audited the $127.51M loan portfolio, validating structured risk-based pricing models via a documented inverse relationship between interest rates and collateral quality.
* Tracked credit capacity metrics showing a disciplined 51.20% mortgage approval rate, with $12.44M currently utilized out of $27.75M total credit capacity (44.8%).

### ⚙️ Operational Friction & Service Bottlenecks
* Categorized customer feedback to map institutional vulnerabilities. Customer complaints accounted for 33.62% of total feedback.
* Localized complaints to specific branch networks and MasterCard transaction paths, indicating a need for targeted service audits.

### 🛡️ Risk Mitigation & Anomaly Detection
* Established a baseline anomaly rate of 6% across active credit lines.
* Isolated 133 pending anomalous cases requiring immediate operational intervention to reduce regulatory and reputational exposure.

## Tech Stack Deployed
* **Business Intelligence:** Power BI (Star schema optimization, relational modeling, DAX measure calculations, interactive cross-filtering UI/UX)
* **Statistical Computing:** R (Exploratory Data Analysis, anomaly baseline profiling)
* **Data Engineering:** Power Query (ETL pipeline architecture, data purification, format normalization)
